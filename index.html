INSTAGRAM

Portugal football star Cristiano Ronaldo has become the first-ever person to reach 400 million followers on Instagram. The Manchester United striker is only behind Instagram's official handle that has over 460 million followers. Interestingly, Ronaldo was the first person to reach 200 million followers in January 2020, a figure that has doubled in over two years.

FACEBOOK

Cristiano Ronaldo is currently the most-followed individual on Facebook, with over 150 million followers

TWITTER

Portuguese footballer Cristiano Ronaldo is the most-followed sports personality and European on Twitter, with over 99 million followers.
